About Computer Science Department

The Computer Science Department at our university is committed to providing high-quality education and research opportunities in the field of computer science. Our programs

are designed to prepare students for successful careers in industry, academia, and beyond.

Admission Criteria

To be eligible for admission to our Computer Science programs, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Completion of high school education or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA requirement
  • Satisfactory performance in entrance exams (if applicable)

Admission processes may vary for different programs. Please refer to the university's admission portal for specific requirements and procedures.

Degree Program Structure

Our Computer Science degree programs offer a comprehensive curriculum covering various core and elective courses. Below is a general structure:

  • Introduction to Computer Science
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Computer Networks
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Database Management Systems
  • Software Engineering

Students are required to complete a certain number of credit hours to fulfill degree requirements.
